Primary concern areas: Model Risk Data Exposure Explainability

Risk function assessment: The volume of AI use case submissions has increased 40% quarter-over-quarter, outpacing the current review capacity of the AI governance team. Seven submissions involve the use of third-party large language models with access to non-public personal information (NPPI), raising concerns under PIPEDA and internal data classification policy. Of these, three have been conditionally approved pending data residency confirmation from the vendor. Two use cases involving automated underwriting decision support have been escalated to the Model Risk Committee for independent review, as outputs may be relied upon in a manner that requires model validation under OSFI Guideline E-23. The Technology Risk function has recommended a temporary intake freeze on Tier 1 AI use cases until a formal AI risk framework, currently in draft, receives sign-off from the Chief Risk Officer and is socialized with the Audit Committee.
