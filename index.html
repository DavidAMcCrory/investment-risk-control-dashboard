Risk function assessment: The volume of operational risk events and control exceptions has increased 40% quarter-over-quarter, concentrated in private markets and alternative investment workflows where manual processing remains prevalent. The Risk function has recommended a focused review of end-to-end controls across the private markets lifecycle, with findings to be presented to the Audit Committee at its next scheduled meeting.
